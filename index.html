Welcome to

Hair Haven

A Sanctuary of Beauty in Mbizo 19

For over 8 years, Hair Haven has been the premier destination for discerning clients in Kwekwe. Founded by Mai Tanatswa, our salon combines timeless techniques with contemporary artistry to create looks that celebrate your individuality.

Beyond our salon services, we're passionate about nurturing the next generation of hairstylists. Our academy offers comprehensive training programs that transform passion into profession, equipping students with both technical excellence and business acumen.
