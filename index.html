About Hair Haven

Your Beauty Destination

Founded by Mai Tanatswa, Hair Haven has been transforming looks and building careers in Kwekwe for over 8 years. We're more than a salon – we're a creative community where artistry meets education.

Our philosophy is simple: exceptional hair care combined with genuine warmth. Whether you're here for a stunning new style or to start your journey as a stylist, you'll find expert guidance every step of the way.

— Mai Tanatswa
