About Hair Haven

Where Passion Meets Precision

Our mission is to empower individuals through exceptional hair services and comprehensive training programs. Whether you're looking for a stunning new look or a career in hairstyling, we're here to guide you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book in advance?

Yes, we recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance, especially for weekends. You can book via phone or our online form.

What products do you use?

We use premium, salon-quality products that are gentle on hair and deliver long-lasting results. Brands include Olaplex, Redken, and our own custom blends.

How long is the training program?

Our comprehensive course runs for 3 months (full-time) or 6 months (part-time), covering theory, practical, and business modules.

Do you offer bridal packages?

Absolutely! We specialize in bridal hair and offer group bookings for bridal parties. Contact us for a consultation.
